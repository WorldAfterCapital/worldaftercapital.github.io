Humans have dreamed of a world of plenty in which nobody needs to work. Imagine you didn’t have to work at a job to support yourself. What would you do with your time? How would your life be different? World After Capital is an open e-book that explores this idea – a small contribution to our distinctly human endeavor, the pursuit of knowledge.
